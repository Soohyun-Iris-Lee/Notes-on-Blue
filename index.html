Notes on Blue is artist Moyra Davey’s lyrical film essay, created during a period of partial blindness. It interweaves her reflections on Derek Jarman’s film Blue, a constant monochromatic screen based on his experience of blindness and the blue light in his vision, with Davey’s own perspective to examine the intersections of blindness, color, and identity.

As part of translating her film into a book publication, this website supplements the project by creating an interactive digital library of the film and interviews, lectures, poems, and books referenced throughout her essay, while experimenting with the perceptual boundary between what is seen and unseen.
